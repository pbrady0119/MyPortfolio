Playing piano and writing code are alike!

During COVID, like most people, I was looking for a hobby to occupy my newly found free-time. I decided to give piano a try. After hours of practice I realized that playing piano and coding are incredibly similar. They both take significant investment of time, attention to detail, and most of all, repetition! The satisfaction I get from solving an issue or fixing a bug in code is just like the feeling I get when I finally learn a difficult section of a song and it's what keeps me coming back for more!
